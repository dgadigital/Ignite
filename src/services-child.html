Our dedicated team has more than forty years of combined experience providing government recruitment services to the public sector.

The public sector has unique and specific compliance requirements, which is why we maintain up-to-date licenses, security clearances, sit on various panels and rigorous processes to ensure that we can deliver the highest level of service to our clients.

Our extensive expertise in this area enables us to provide you with the best possible talent to achieve your organisational objectives. We understand the complexities of the legislative framework that underpins roles in the public sector as well as the importance of equity and diversity to create a well-rounded and potent team. Our achievements and impact

Nationwide desktop refresh for an Australian police department
Ignite created and managed the deployment of 13,500 EUC devices across 400 locations for an Australian police department. Utilising 70 resources, we delivered this project on time and on budget.

On-site technical support for office products retailer
Our on-demand IT services provided on-site technical support for an office products retailer. We provided dispatch personnel as well as additional IT FTEs to deliver IMAC and ticket-based support across VIC, NSW, QLD and WA.

National infrastructure support for office products retailer
Our team delivered national infrastructure support for a Tier-1 systems integrator at multiple data centres across Australia. We supplied more than 100 IT operational staff to provide national system server operations plus outsourcing services for over 15 years.
