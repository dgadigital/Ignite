About Ignite

With over 70 employees across 5 offices in Australia, Ignite has a long and rich unrivalled history with great clients spanning 40 years. Ignite has worked hard to establish itself as an organisation offering genuine support in a high-performing culture that people are enthusiastic to participate in. And now it’s your turn to be welcomed. 
Hear what our employee have to say
“I have worked at Ignite for almost a decade as an IT recruitment consultant in our Melbourne team. I’m given lots of autonomy in how I run my desk and provided with the tools and support I need to do it effectively. Ignite isn’t the largest recruiter out there, but we have a close-knit and collaborative team which manifests a strong internal culture. I love working at Ignite and am excited for our future growth.”
Senior Recruitment Consultant IT & Digital (VIC)
“I have been working in the On Demand team for over a year. On Demand provides us with a conducive and employee-friendly work environment, which enables me to provide high quality service to our candidates and clients. All individuals are well supported and I have a very healthy relationship with my manager. Working with the Resource On Demand team has been a great experience, a complete package within itself!”
Resources On Demand Consultant – On Demand (NSW)
