What Does a Recruitment Consultant Do?

This role is designed to secure new business clients, as well as retain and develop existing clients by promoting Ignite products and services. This role is accountable for providing clients with suitable contract and permanent staff and effectively managing our candidates and contractors. The role also requires the incumbent to uncover opportunities for Ignite to deploy the breadth of available products and services. Account Managers must meet monthly targets that are linked to revenue generation as a result of permanent and contract placements.

What the day brings

Recruiters will aim to accurately define responsibilities and required competencies of roles to identify the key candidate requirements for the sourcing process. They locate, attract, and retain skilled candidates through proactive recruiting and engagement techniques. they will also make valid and reliable judgements to endorse and recommend candidates for hire.

The win

Recruiter will aim to keep stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the recruitment process. It is important to cultivate relationships as a trusted advisor through consulting and closing on roles with both candidates and hiring managers.

Additionally, recuirters will generate and cultivate sales leads by pursuing sales pipeline and engagement channels.
