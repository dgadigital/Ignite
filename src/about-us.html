About us

Ignite your workforce

As our name suggests, we're the spark that sets your bottom line alight by matching outstanding talent to your needs and culture.

As a publicly listed (ASX: IGN) recruitment agency with four decades of experience, Ignite is dedicated to delivering exceptional recruitment, across Australia's public and private sectors.

The Ignite difference is key. We devote ourselves to creating and maintaining long-term client relationships through ongoing support well after placing the right candidate. And it pays off. Our clients come back to us time and time again because they know we are leaders in recruitment. Some of our clients have been with us for thirty years.

Plus, we've established an exemplary performance track record and marketplace standing. With our advanced methodologies, deep industry knowledge, and strong stakeholder relationships, we have earned a reputation as Australia's trusted talent advisor.
