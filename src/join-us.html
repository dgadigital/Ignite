Ignite your recruitment career

The people who comprise Ignite are our most valuable asset and create a powerhouse of a recruitment agency.

We provide unrivalled service and support to our clients and candidates and are always keen to welcome more passionate people to do so.

When you join our team, you’re more than just an employee. You’re part of a formidable team that values growth, learning, and unlimited potential and genuinely makes a difference in the lives of others.

Our Values

Connection

We create enduring connections between talent and organisations. We understand the needs of all our stakeholders and build strong relationships to ignite their potential.

Ingenuity

As lateral thinkers who seek out innovative approaches, we prioritise creativity, diversity and originality, whether it’s in daily tasks or big projects.

Courage

By daring to be different, our boldness and honesty power our actions, leading to accumulative success and delighted clients and stakeholders.

Understanding

Through a consistently compassionate approach, our clients, candidates and team members benefit from genuine empathy and thoughtful interactions.

About Ignite

With over 70 employees across 5 offices in Australia, Ignite has a long and rich unrivalled history with great clients spanning 40 years. Ignite has worked hard to establish itself as an organisation offering genuine support in a high-performing culture that people are enthusiastic to participate in. And now it’s your turn to be welcomed.
