Construction Recruitment

For nearly forty years, Ignite has specialised in construction recruitment, providing contract, temporary and permanent recruitment solutions at all levels of private and public construction businesses. 

With many Australian states experiencing an infrastructure boom, hiring the right candidates for construction roles has never been more important. And even though the construction industry is expanding and changing rapidly, the inherent need for people power remains the same. 

Our time-tested approach ensures you are connected with the right candidates with the right experience for the role. Providing a level of seamlessness to recruitment in the fast-paced world of construction. 

Our network gives you the advantage

Not only have we been in business for forty years, supplying some of the most notable public and private organisations in Australia, but we have vigorously built a sizeable network. 

Our extensive contractor network extends to both the active and passive recruitment markets. This unprecedented whole-of-market approach means our clients are connected with higher-quality professionals. 

By taking the time to cultivate relationships and provide ongoing support post-placement, we have a deeper understanding of what motivates job seekers. All of which work together to lay the platform for achieving better outcomes. As people are at the fore of all construction processes and practices, we consistently work to provide your business with the right talent. Whether that talent is needed on a permanent, temporary or contract basis. 

Through providing the right professional and cultural fit for your organisation, the recruitment process is streamlined, helping your business operations remain efficient and effective.

Short- and long-term construction hires

Whether you require talent on a short- or long-term basis, our dedicated consultants employ strategic recruitment solutions and advanced methodology that provide real results. 

With construction projects regularly commencing or scaling up, the right hires can provide both short- and long-term benefits. In the short term, our candidates will ensure your business operations can proceed smoothly. In the long term, an expertly matched candidate can improve both business efficiencies and supplier relationships. Providing your construction business with a competitive advantage. 

Exceptional consultants

Recruiting skilled professionals is in our nature. Our consultants have a unique mix of in-field and recruitment experience and, above all else, value connection. With unparalleled recruitment industry experience, their market knowledge and expertise combine to garner the best results for your business. 

Employing a unique mix of expertise, passion and ingenuity, our consultants strive to relate to clients, contractors and the employment experience. This approach leads to a deeper understanding of your company, the role and what constitutes the right hire.
